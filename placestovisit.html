The Anchor Coffee House

The Anchor is a lovely independent coffee house located next to Withworth Park. At Anchor they serve Monmouth coffee and have a great range of bagels, waffles and snacks available. Anchor coffee House is part of the Suspended Coffee movement. When buying food or drink you can add a suspended coffee to your order, that money is then spent on providing coffee/food at community projects they run.

Address: 508 Moss Lane East, M14 4PA

Opening hours:
Monday-Friday 9am-5pm
Saturday 10am-4pm
Sunday closed


Takk

Takk is a Scandinavian artisan coffee house in Manchester's Northern Qurter, serving a wonderful Nordic style espresso from Clifton Coffee Roasters in Bristol, which is also available to buy in bags at the counter. Takk aim to provide a welcoming space to work, meet, talk and dream. They also offer a great range of simple and delicious food, from full meals to small snacks.

Address: 6 Tariff Street, M1 2FF

Opening hours:
Monday-Friday 8am-5pm
Saturday 9am-5pm
Sunday 10am-5pm


Grindsmith

Grindsmith serve amazing coffee at several sites in the city center. They have a coffee 'pod' at Greengate square which is unique but small and a coffee shop on Deansgate which is much bigger with a lovely atmosphere and space to enjoy a snack with your coffee. Grindsmith use Manchester roasters Heart and Graft, widely recognised as the best loacal roasted with other snacks and baked goods sources locally.

Address: Shop: 231-233 Deansgate Manchester, M3 4EN
Pod: Greengate Square, Victoria Bridge Street, M3 5AS

Opening Hours:
Monday-Saturday: 8am-5pm
Sunday: 9am-5pm


Pot Kettle Black

Pot Kettle Black is an artisan coffee shop in Deansgate with a health-concious edge. Set up by two St Helen's rugby players, their passion for coffee and getting that perfect cup thoroughly shines through. To accompany your coffee there is a great range of healthy and nutritious food and a range of delicious cakes too if you're after that sweet sweet treat. They also run a high intensity fitness session on Tuesday and Thursday mornings, perfect for people wanting to fit in a morning exercise session and still get to enjoy that morning coffee!

Address: Barton Arcade, Deansgate, M3 2bW

Opening Hours:
Monday-Friday: 8am-7pm
Saturday: 9am-6pm
Sunday: 10am-5pm
